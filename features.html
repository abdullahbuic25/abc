Uniquely Crafted Platform Features

Built for researchers
By researchers

Terapage offers researchers a seamless experience, enabling
them to effortlessly conduct qualitative research
projects across the globe. Equipped with a comprehensive
array of features for both qualitative and quantitative
data collection, moderation, and analysis, researchers
can swiftly establish an online presence and begin
fieldwork within a mere day.

Get Started

Seamlessly gain
valuable insights

Hone your skills in mixed methods research by seamlessly integrating qualitative and quantitative responses. TeraPage empowers you with a diverse toolkit, including features like open-ended text, photo and video uploads, image and video markup for quantitative data derived from card sorting and ranking tasks, as well as scale and allocation questions, single or multiple choice polls, grids, and a wealth of other options. Unlock valuable insights as you embark on your research journey with us.

Discover more

Customize your
research approach

Shape your study to cater to diverse participant segments by employing various methods such as asynchronous activities or discussions, real-time chat, and live video interviews. With TeraPage, you have the flexibility to define sequences, set logical conditions, and seamlessly integrate with other research tools. Tailor your research design to unlock meaningful insights from different perspectives and enhance the overall quality of your study.

Discover more

Facilitate moderation
and foster collaboration

Engage participants by creating a social environment for their responses. Spark interactive discussions by asking open or private probing questions based on the received responses. Encourage deeper group conversation using mentions (@) to involve specific individuals. Stay adaptable throughout your research process by seamlessly incorporating new activities as your study progresses. TeraPage provides you with the tools to moderate effectively, encourage socialization, and continually adapt to the evolving needs of your research.

Discover more

Effortlessly handle
participant management

Simplify the process of measuring and reporting on the health of the community and activity progress to effectively manage your participants. Keep track of participation and completion rates, streamline communication by automating follow-up notifications, and promptly address any necessary follow-ups manually. With TeraPage, you can easily identify participants deserving of rewards through our pre-built reports, ensuring a seamless and efficient management experience.

Discover More

Conduct analysis and
generate insightful reports

TeraPage provides powerful tools to filter data based on activities, segments, or individuals. Dive into response streams, examine charts and data tables, and delve into word clouds to swiftly identify crucial themes. Foster collaboration by saving and coding significant excerpts, while leveraging automated video transcripts and highlighted clips for comprehensive analysis. Instantly download curated content and essential information with a simple click, enabling you to generate reports efficiently and derive valuable insights from your research.

Discover More

Embrace mobility and
expand your reach globally

Terapage offers a seamless and optimized user experience across all mobile devices, eliminating the need for app downloads. With support for over 25 languages, both participants and moderators can engage comfortably in their preferred language. Choose from a range of data hosting options in the USA, Canada, Europe, Asia, United Kingdom, Africa, South America and Australia, ensuring compliance with regional regulations. Additionally, TeraPage provides customizable security settings, allowing you to configure private and enhanced security setups based on your specific needs. Embrace the mobile revolution and unlock global accessibility with TeraPage's user-friendly and language-diverse platform.
