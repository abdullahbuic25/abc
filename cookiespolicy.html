Cookies Policy


Welcome to the Terapage Cookie Policy. At Terapage Limited, we value your privacy and strive to enhance your experience on our website Terapage.com (referred to as the "Site") through the use of cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, and other tracking technologies. This Cookie Policy outlines how we utilize these technologies and the data they collect.

We reserve the right to modify and update this Cookie Policy as needed. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated version on the Site, indicated by the "Last Updated" date. By continuing to use the Site after such changes, you acknowledge and accept the revised Cookie Policy without the need for individual notice.

We encourage you to review this Cookie Policy periodically to stay informed about any updates. Your continued use of the Site following the posting of changes signifies your awareness of and agreement to the revised Cookie Policy.


Use of Cookies

Cookies are strings of information that assign a unique identifier to your computer, which is stored by your browser. Each time you interact with the Site, your browser provides this identifier. We utilize cookies on the Site for various purposes, such as tracking services used, recording registration information, storing user preferences, facilitating purchases, and analyzing page visits. Cookies assist us in understanding how the Site is used and improving your overall user experience.


Types of Cookies

When you visit the Site, the following types of cookies may be employed:

Advertising Cookies: These cookies are placed on your computer by advertisers and ad servers to display relevant advertisements based on your interests. They gather information about your visits to the Site and other websites, customize ads for specific computers, and track ad views and engagement. No personal information is collected by these cookies.

Analytics Cookies: Analytics cookies track how users reach and navigate the Site, providing insights into the most effective features and areas for improvement.

Personalization Cookies: These cookies recognize returning visitors, recording their browsing history, visited pages, settings, and preferences to enhance their experience on the Site.

Security Cookies: Security cookies assist in identifying and preventing security risks, helping to authenticate users and safeguard user data from unauthorized access.

Site Management Cookies: Site management cookies maintain user identity and session information, preventing unexpected logouts and retaining entered information across different pages. While individual disabling is not possible, you can disable all cookies in your browser settings.


Based on Responsibility:

Internal Cookies: Our "first-party cookies" are necessary for the proper functioning and provision of essential features and functionalities on the Site. They can be either permanent or temporary, and disabling them may impact Site functionality.

Third-Party Cookies: Third-party cookies are placed by companies that provide specific services on the Site. They enable those third parties to gather information about you. You can manually disable these cookies in your browser.


Control of Cookies

Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies automatically. However, you have the option to remove or reject cookies through your browser's settings. It's important to note that such actions may affect the availability and functionality of the Site. For detailed instructions on managing cookies, please refer to your browser or device's settings.


Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may employ web beacons, pixel tags, and other tracking technologies to customize the Site and enhance your experience. These technologies, such as tiny objects or images embedded in web pages or emails, help track page visits, email views, and collect statistical data. They gather limited information, such as a cookie number, timestamp, and page or email description. While you cannot decline web beacons and pixel tags specifically, you can control their use by managing associated cookies.


Privacy Policy

For more details on how we utilize information collected by cookies and other tracking technologies, please review our Privacy Policy, which is incorporated into this Cookie Policy. By using the Site, you agree to be bound by both this Cookie Policy and our Privacy Policy.
