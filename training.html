Get Started

Our levels
of training

Immerse yourself in one of the most advanced comprehensive online research platform. Recognizing that different needs require different solutions, we offer three levels of training tailored to your study and role and specific areas of expertise. Take a deep dive and choose the training that best suits your goals and aspirations.

  • Beginner: Train to use the basics of the platform and get on with your research.
  • Intermediate: Train to use the most of the features of the platform and get more out of your research.
  • Expert: Train to use the all the features of the platform and get the best out of your research.
  • Custom: Please, get in touch to discuss your training needs.
See our packages

Why you should choose
our training service

Training signifies that you possess the knowledge to use Terapage at a level comparable to our in-house consultants. Each training level offers distinct advantages, such as:

  • Expect reduced programming time, decreased reliance on Terapage for QuickStarts, lower costs, and optimized returns on your technology investment.
  • Streamline study management, leading to enhanced engagement, quicker insights, and more impactful analysis and reporting.
  • Apply to become a preferred partner, provide hands-on support to participants, and deliver an exceptional level of service to clients by conducting follow-ups directly within Terapage.

What you should
already know

Includes a strong foundation in research and methods. Whilst familiarity with online tools is highly recommended. The training program covers a comprehensive aspects of research, methods and approach, it is mostly aimed at aiding the understanding of Terapage training sessions for those who are new to the platform.

See our packages

How the training
is delivered

This is conveniently delivered online through the Terapage platform. You will engage with videos, images, and interactive articles, and each course is followed by a quiz and a brief exercise. Your overall proficiency will be evaluated through a series of knowledge check activity. A certificate of participation would be available for download upon successfully completing the training.

See our packages

Expected time
commitment

The time commitment for the training is flexible. If you solely concentrate on the courses, it typically takes around 3 to 5 days to absorb the knowledge, complete exercises, and finish the knowledge check. However, we understand that researchers often have busy schedules. To ensure optimal learning, we recommend spreading the courses over a period of 2 to 3 weeks, accommodating your availability and allowing for a comprehensive understanding of the material.

See our packages

Retraining &
staying abreast

Maintaining your expertise is important to us and we understand that life can be busy. That is why we have a retraining option to ensure that you stay up-to-date with the latest advancements. Throughout your training period, you'll enjoy complimentary access to any newly released training courses. When your training feels like it is out of date, you have the option to re-train at a discounted rate and complete any outstanding updated courses to keep your skills current. We are committed to supporting your continuous professional growth.
