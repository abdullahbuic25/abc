Extra help exactly

Whether you prefer a "Collaborative Research Effort" (CRE) approach

Get Started

Independent researcher
package

Ideal for tech-savvy researchers who prefer a hands-on approach, our platform-only option provides you with the tools and resources to run your projects seamlessly from start to finish. We offer comprehensive training on setup, moderation, and reporting, along with in-app help articles, email, chat, and phone support. To ensure optimal performance, we conduct a pre-launch check, guaranteeing that your study is set up for success.

See our packages

Quick launch
package

Designed for teams in need of extra setup assistance, our QuickStart package includes all the standard Platform Only services, with the added benefit of our support in reviewing and programming your activity guide. We will also assist you in uploading your participants and sending out invitations, ensuring a smooth and efficient process. With QuickStart, you'll have the support you need to get your research project up and running quickly and effectively.

Tailored research
services

Looking for additional support? Our dedicated team of in-house research experts is here to assist you every step of the way. Whether you need guidance on your research project or community setup, advice on best practices, or strategic consultation, our experts have the knowledge and experience to help you achieve success. Additionally, if you require full-service support, we can seamlessly coordinate with our certified partners to deliver comprehensive solutions tailored to your specific needs. With our research services, you can confidently navigate your research journey and maximize your outcomes.

Collaborative research
effort (CRE)

Looking to engage with your target audience through an immersive and ongoing research community? If you're unsure where to begin, our "Collaborative Research Effort" (CRE) approach is the perfect solution. With our collaborative model, you'll have access to comprehensive technology and service support while maintaining an active role in the research process. Together, we'll navigate the journey, ensuring you have the necessary resources and expertise to drive meaningful insights. Join forces with us and unlock the full potential of your research community.
