Nurturing mutual growth

Since its inception in 2020, Terapage has been serving independent researchers, starting as a simple do-it-yourself software service. As we have expanded to cater to a wider audience, we have remained committed to the core values that matter to smaller firms – personalized support and adaptable solutions. We understand the importance of direct assistance and pragmatic flexibility, and we offer a range of resources to foster our mutual growth:

  • Comprehensive moderator training and certification courses.
  • Dedicated programming support to streamline your projects.
  • Flexible payment terms tailored to your financial needs.
  • Collaboration opportunities for mutually beneficial partnerships.
